Server Rules

Rules are here to keep everyone safe. Disobeying these rules will cause warnings and moderation. Your presence in the discord server means that you read and agree to the rules, regardless if you read them. Any moderation can be taken regardless if it is a rule or not.

Rules:

1. No fighting in chat
2. Listen to all moderators and staff
3. Don't ping mods/staff/admin/devs/owners for no reason
4. No cursing
5. No JFJ (Join for Join)
6. Wait for your bot to be reviewed
7. Don't mini-mod.
8. No NSFW or obscene content. This includes text, images, or links featuring nudity, sex, hard violence, or other graphically disturbing content.
9. No spam or self-promotion (server invites, advertisements, etc) without permission from a staff member. This includes DMing fellow members.
10. Inviting, using, or coding a bot with commands to destroy the server like nuking, will result in a immediate ban
